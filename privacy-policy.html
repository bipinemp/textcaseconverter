1. Information We Collect

Text Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any text data that you enter into our tool. All text processing happens entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device.

Usage Analytics

We may collect basic, anonymous usage statistics such as:

  • Page views and general usage patterns
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility purposes)
  • General geographic location (country level only)
  • Referral sources (which websites link to us)

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect your text data, we cannot use it in any way. The limited analytics data we may collect is used solely for:

  • Improving the performance and functionality of our tool
  • Understanding general usage patterns to guide development
  • Ensuring compatibility across different browsers and devices
  • Monitoring for technical issues and security threats

3. Data Storage and Security

Your Text Data

Your text data is processed entirely in your browser's memory and is automatically cleared when you:

  • Close the browser tab or window
  • Navigate away from the page
  • Refresh the page
  • Use the "Clear All" button

Website Security

Our website uses HTTPS encryption to ensure secure communication between your browser and our servers. However, since all processing happens client-side, there's minimal data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Web Hosting: To serve the website files
  • Analytics: To understand general usage patterns (if implemented)
  • Content Delivery: To ensure fast loading times globally

These services may have their own privacy policies, but they do not have access to your text data since it never leaves your browser.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our tool does not use cookies to store personal information. We may use:

  • Session Storage: To temporarily remember your preferences during your visit
  • Local Storage: To save user interface preferences (like theme settings) locally on your device

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy. However, we recommend that children use the internet under appropriate supervision.

7. International Users

Since all processing happens in your browser and we don't collect personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Users from any country can use our tool with the same level of privacy protection.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, traditional data rights (like access, deletion, or portability) don't apply. However, you have complete control over your data because:

  • You can clear your text at any time using the "Clear All" button
  • Your data is automatically cleared when you leave the page
  • You can disable JavaScript to prevent any processing (though this will break the tool's functionality)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be highlighted on our website
  • We'll maintain the same core principle of not collecting your text data

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us. We're committed to transparency and will respond to your inquiries promptly.

11. Open Source Commitment

Our tool is built with standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and operates transparently. You can inspect the source code in your browser's developer tools to verify that no data is being transmitted.

Key Takeaway

Your privacy is our priority. We've designed TextConverter to work entirely in your browser, ensuring that your text data remains completely private and under your control at all times.
