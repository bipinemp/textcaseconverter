Frequently Asked Questions

Is TextConverter really free?

Yes! TextConverter is completely free to use with no limitations, no registration required, and no hidden fees.

Do you store my text data?

No, we don't store any of your text data. All processing happens in your browser and your text never leaves your device.

Can I use this tool offline?

Once the page is loaded, the tool works offline since all processing happens in your browser.

What browsers are supported?

TextConverter works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Can I suggest new text case formats?

We're always looking to add new useful text case formats. Send us your suggestions!

Is there an API available?

Currently, we only offer the web interface. However, if there's enough interest, we might consider creating an API in the future.
